How it works

From order to spoon

1

You order

Pick a signature flavour or build your own. The order goes straight to my Telegram — I confirm within a few hours.

2

I churn it

Made fresh in small batches, usually within 24–48 hours. Tea is brewed gong-fu strength, never from powders or syrups.

3

I deliver

Personally, frozen, in 1 / 2 / 5 kg packs. We agree a time slot that suits you.

4

You pay

On delivery: cash, card, Revolut or crypto. If something's wrong with the order — you don't pay.
